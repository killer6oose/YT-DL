Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, username, email, and password (hashed).
  • Download History: Tracked only for logged-in users.
  • Session Data: Stored temporarily for authentication.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • We use your data to manage accounts
  • To help improve service security
  • Track download history (if logged in)
    - For you to download previously downloaded audio/videos
    - For when you inevitably forget WHAT you have/have not downloaded

3. Data Retention

Your data is retained until you delete your account. Once deleted, all associated data is permanently erased within 24 hours.

Data that will be deleted upon account deletion:
  • Username
  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Download History

4. Security

  • All passwords are stored securely using encryption.
  • We do not share or sell your data.

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. A session cookie is used only for authentication.

6. Third-Party Services

We may integrate APIs, but we do not share user data with third parties.

7. Your Rights

You may delete your account at any time, which will erase all stored data.

To delete your data you must navigate to the Account Page and select "Delete Account" at the bottom of the page.

8. Contact Information

For inquiries, email us at support@cronotech.us.

For support, you may use that email address or contact us via the Contact page.
